OUR STORY

Whether you are craving a comforting classic to remind you of a home-cooked meal or an exciting taste of local cuisine, Kanthari Restaurant and Banquet has the ideal dining option to suit your needs.

Kanthari Restaurant and Banquet is made for caring hospitality. We are well known for feeding the minds, bodies and soul of our guests through an attitude of serving tasty food.

About Banquet

Kanthari Restaurant and Banquet Restaurant does not offer typical banquet room but instead featured distinctive spacious banquet to accommodate company meetings, outings and all events.
